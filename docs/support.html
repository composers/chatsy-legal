Chatsy Support

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Chatsy is a free iOS chat app that uses on-device Apple Intelligence. This page explains how to get help.

Before you contact us

  • Use iOS 26 or later on a device that supports Apple Intelligence.
  • Turn on Apple Intelligence in Settings → Apple Intelligence (system settings).
  • If your device does not support Apple Intelligence, Chatsy offers demo mode with placeholder responses only.
  • For privacy details, see the Privacy Policy.

Common issues

  • Chat does not respond — Confirm Apple Intelligence is enabled and wait if the model is still downloading. Try force-quitting and reopening Chatsy.
  • Voice input does not work — Allow microphone and speech recognition when prompted. Voice input only works while you hold the voice button.
  • “AI NOT SUPPORTED” screen — Your device cannot run Apple Intelligence. Tap DEMO to preview the UI only.
  • Clear saved chat — Start a new chat in the app, or delete Chatsy from your device to remove all local data.

Contact

For bugs, feedback, or support requests, open an issue on GitHub. Include your device model, iOS version, and whether Apple Intelligence is enabled.
