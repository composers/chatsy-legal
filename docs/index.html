Chatsy Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Who we are

Chatsy is published by composers (the developer of the Chatsy iOS app). This policy describes how the Chatsy app handles information on your device.

For privacy questions, open an issue on GitHub or contact us through the support channel listed on the app’s App Store page.

Summary

Chatsy runs on your device. Chat text and voice input stay on your iPhone or iPad. We do not operate our own servers for conversations and we do not receive your messages, voice audio, or chat history.

Requirements

Chatsy requires iOS 26 or later and a device that supports Apple Intelligence with Apple Intelligence enabled in Settings. On devices without Apple Intelligence, the app offers a demo mode with placeholder responses only (see below).

On-device processing and Apple services

Messages are processed with Apple Intelligence (Foundation Models) on your device. Apple controls model availability, updates, and related system behavior under Apple’s Privacy Policy.

We do not control Apple’s systems. When you use voice input, speech recognition is provided by Apple’s Speech framework. Chatsy requests on-device recognition when your device supports it; if it is not available, Apple’s speech services may apply according to Apple’s policies.

AI-generated content

Chatsy uses on-device Apple Intelligence for chat. Responses are generated automatically and may be inaccurate, incomplete, or inappropriate. Do not rely on Chatsy for medical, legal, financial, or emergency advice.

Data we access on your device

  • Messages you send and AI replies — processed on-device for chat; not sent to us.
  • Microphone and speech recognition — only while you hold the voice button, to fill the message field. When supported, recognition runs on-device.
  • Saved chat — stored locally under Application Support (excluded from device backups such as iCloud Backup or encrypted computer backup) so your conversation can be restored after you close Chatsy. Stored with file protection when your device is locked.
  • App settings — appearance and mood preferences stored locally (UserDefaults).

Demo mode

If your device does not support Apple Intelligence, you may use demo mode. Demo responses are placeholder text generated in the app only. They are not sent to us and are not processed by Apple Intelligence.

Data we do not collect

Chatsy does not include analytics, advertising, or account sign-in. We do not sell your data and we do not collect personal information on our servers because we do not operate chat servers.

How to delete your data

  • Start a new chat in the app to replace your saved conversation.
  • Delete the app from your device to remove all local Chatsy data.

Children

Chatsy is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Material changes may also be described in App Store release notes.
