Chatsy Privacy Policy

Summary

Chatsy runs on your device. Chat text and voice input stay on your iPhone or iPad. We do not operate our own servers for conversations.

On-device processing

Messages are processed with Apple Intelligence (Foundation Models). Apple handles model availability and updates according to its policies.

Data we access

  • Microphone and speech recognition — only while you hold the voice button, to fill the message field. When your device supports it, recognition runs on-device.
  • Saved chat — stored locally on your device (excluded from iCloud and iTunes/Finder backups) so your conversation can be restored after you close Chatsy.

Data we do not collect

Chatsy does not include analytics, advertising, or account sign-in. We do not sell your data.
